SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

Minimum Requirements:
- Android 7.0 (Nougat, API Level 24) or higher
- 100 MB free storage space
- Internet connection (for initial setup and content download)
- Google account (for sign-in)

Recommended Requirements:
- Android 10.0 or higher
- 200 MB free storage space
- Stable Wi-Fi connection for video content
- 2 GB RAM minimum

================================================================================
INSTALLATION METHODS
================================================================================

METHOD 1: Install from APK File

1. Download the Amoxcalli APK file to your Android device
   - Transfer the APK to your device if downloaded on computer

2. Enable installation from unknown sources:
   - Go to Settings > Security (or Privacy)
   - Enable "Install unknown apps" or "Unknown sources"
   - Select your file manager/browser and allow installation

3. Locate the APK file:
   - Open your file manager
   - Navigate to Downloads folder
   - Tap on "amoxcalli-app.apk"

4. Install the application:
   - Tap "Install" when prompted
   - Wait for installation to complete
   - Tap "Open" to launch the app

5. (Optional) Disable unknown sources:
   - Return to Settings > Security
   - Disable "Install unknown apps" for security


METHOD 2: Install from Google Play Store

1. Open Google Play Store on your Android device
2. Search for "Amoxcalli LSM"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Wait for download and installation to complete
5. Tap "Open" to launch the app


================================================================================
FIRST-TIME SETUP
================================================================================

1. Launch Amoxcalli:
   - Tap the app icon from your home screen or app drawer

2. Sign in with Google:
   - Tap "Continuar con Google" (Continue with Google)
   - Select your Google account
   - Grant permissions when requested:
     * Account information access
     * Network access

3. Welcome screen:
   - Read the brief introduction to LSM learning
   - Tap "Comenzar" (Start) to proceed

4. Initial data sync:
   - Wait for your user profile to load
   - Learning units and content will download
   - This may take 30-60 seconds on first launch

5. Home screen:
   - You'll see your learning dashboard
   - Your account is now ready to use!

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Installation Issues:
--------------------

Problem: "App not installed" error
Solution:
  - Ensure you have enough storage space (100MB+)
  - Try uninstalling any previous version
  - Clear cache: Settings > Apps > Amoxcalli > Clear Cache

Problem: Installation blocked by Play Protect
Solution:
  - Tap "More details" in the warning
  - Select "Install anyway"
  - Note: Official releases are safe

Problem: APK file won't open
Solution:
  - Verify the APK file is not corrupted (re-download if needed)
  - Ensure you're using Android 7.0 or higher
  - Try a different file manager app


First Launch Issues:
--------------------

Problem: Stuck on loading screen
Solution:
  - Check your internet connection
  - Close and restart the app
  - Clear app data: Settings > Apps > Amoxcalli > Clear Data

Problem: Google Sign-In fails
Solution:
  - Verify you have Google Play Services installed
  - Check that your Google account is active
  - Ensure date/time settings are correct
  - Try signing in with a different Google account

Problem: Videos won't load
Solution:
  - Verify stable internet connection (Wi-Fi recommended)
  - Check if your network blocks video CDN (Cloudflare R2)
  - Wait a few moments and try again
  - Restart the app

Problem: App crashes on startup
Solution:
  - Restart your device
  - Ensure Android OS is updated
  - Uninstall and reinstall the app
  - Check minimum requirements (Android 7.0+)


Network Issues:
---------------

Problem: Content won't download
Solution:
  - Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data
  - Disable VPN if active
  - Check firewall settings
  - Ensure backend server is accessible:
    https://ketzallidbapi-production.up.railway.app/api/


================================================================================
UPDATING THE APP
================================================================================

APK Updates:
1. Download the latest APK version
2. Install over the existing app (data will be preserved)
3. Your progress and settings will remain intact

Google Play Updates:
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Go to "My apps & games"
3. Find Amoxcalli and tap "Update"


================================================================================
UNINSTALLATION
================================================================================

To uninstall Amoxcalli:

1. Go to Settings > Apps > Amoxcalli
2. Tap "Uninstall"
3. Confirm uninstallation

Note: Your learning progress is saved on the server. If you reinstall and
sign in with the same Google account, your progress will be restored.
