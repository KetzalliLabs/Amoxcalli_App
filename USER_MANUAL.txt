8. TIPS & BEST PRACTICES
================================================================================

Learning Strategies:
--------------------
âœ“ Practice daily, even if only 5-10 minutes
âœ“ Use slow-motion feature to study hand shapes closely
âœ“ Mimic signs physically while watching (muscle memory)
âœ“ Review previously learned signs regularly (spaced repetition)
âœ“ Focus on understanding hand position, movement, and facial expression
âœ“ Practice in front of a mirror when possible
âœ“ Use Library to preview upcoming lessons
âœ“ Don't rush - mastery over speed

Effective Study Sessions:
--------------------------
1. Start with 2-3 lessons (10-15 min)
2. Take a quick break
3. Do 5-10 practice exercises
4. Review difficult signs in Library
5. End with a quiz for bonus XP

Maintaining Streaks:
--------------------
- Set a daily reminder notification
- Do at least 1 exercise before bed
- Use offline mode when traveling
- Keep app installed and visible on home screen

Video Learning Tips:
--------------------
- Watch each sign 3-5 times before answering
- Pay attention to:
  * Hand shape (finger positions)
  * Movement direction and speed
  * Location (near face, chest, etc.)
  * Facial expressions (critical in LSM!)
- Use slow-motion for complex signs
- Replay as many times as needed - no penalty

Troubleshooting Difficult Signs:
---------------------------------
1. Watch video in slow motion (ðŸŒ)
2. Pause and observe hand position
3. Check Library for additional context
4. Practice the physical motion yourself
5. Review similar signs to note differences
6. Revisit in next practice session

9. TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Common Issues & Solutions:
--------------------------

Problem: Videos won't play or buffer constantly
Solution:
  - Check internet connection strength
  - Switch to Wi-Fi if using mobile data
  - Lower video quality: Profile > Settings > Video Quality > 480p
  - Clear cache: Profile > Settings > Clear Cache
  - Close and restart app

Problem: Progress not saving
Solution:
  - Ensure you're connected to internet
  - Check that you're signed in (Profile shows your name)
  - Wait a few seconds after completing lesson
  - Restart app to force sync

Problem: Streak not counting
Solution:
  - Complete at least 1 exercise (not just viewing)
  - Ensure date/time on device is correct
  - Complete exercise before midnight in your timezone
  - Check Profile to verify streak count

Problem: Can't unlock next unit
Solution:
  - Complete all lessons in current unit
  - Pass the unit review with 70% or higher
  - Ensure progress has synced (check internet)
  - Restart app and check again

Problem: App running slow
Solution:
  - Close background apps
  - Clear app cache
  - Ensure 100MB+ free storage
  - Restart device
  - Update to latest app version

Problem: Exercise answers not registering
Solution:
  - Tap answer clearly and wait 1 second
  - Ensure full video has loaded before answering
  - Restart app if issue persists

Problem: Google Sign-In fails
Solution:
  - Check internet connection
  - Ensure Google Play Services is updated
  - Try different Google account
  - Sign out from app and sign back in

10. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Is internet required to use Amoxcalli?
A: Internet is required for initial setup and downloading content. You can
   download units for offline use in Settings > Offline Mode.

Q: How long does it take to complete all units?
A: With daily practice (15-20 min/day), most users complete the core
   curriculum in 3-4 months. Learning pace varies individually.

Q: Can I skip lessons I already know?
A: Each unit has a placement quiz. Pass it to skip directly to the review.
   However, we recommend completing all lessons for comprehensive learning.

Q: What happens if I miss a day and break my streak?
A: Your streak resets to 0, but your XP, level, and learning progress remain.
   Start a new streak the next day you practice.

Q: Can I use Amoxcalli on multiple devices?
A: Yes! Sign in with the same Google account on any device. Your progress
   syncs automatically across devices.

Q: Are the signs standardized LSM?
A: Yes, all signs follow LSM (Lengua de SeÃ±as Mexicana) standards used in
   Mexico. Regional variations exist but core signs are widely recognized.

Q: Can I download all content at once?
A: You can download units individually. Go to Profile > Settings > Offline
   Mode and select units to download. Each unit is approximately 20-50 MB.

Q: How do I report incorrect content or bugs?
A: Go to Profile > Settings > Report a Problem. Describe the issue and
   include the lesson/sign name if applicable.

Q: Will more content be added?
A: Yes! New units, signs, and features are added regularly. Check the Home
   screen for announcements.

Q: Can I reset my progress and start over?
A: Yes, go to Profile > Settings > Advanced > Reset Progress. Note: This
   permanently deletes all learning data and cannot be undone.

Q: What do I do after completing all units?
A: Continue practicing with daily quizzes, explore advanced Library content,
   and help others learn LSM! More advanced units coming soon.

Q: How accurate do my sign movements need to be?
A: The app tests comprehension (recognizing signs) not production (performing
   signs). Practice physical signs separately with mirrors or partners.

LEARNING PHILOSOPHY
================================================================================

Amoxcalli uses proven language learning techniques:
- Spaced Repetition: Review signs at optimal intervals
- Active Recall: Test yourself before seeing answers
- Microlearning: Short, focused lessons for better retention
- Gamification: Stay motivated with rewards and progress tracking
- Visual Learning: Video demonstrations for accurate sign learning
- Immediate Feedback: Know instantly if your answer is correct

Remember: Language learning is a marathon, not a sprint. Consistency beats
intensity. 10 minutes daily is better than 2 hours once a week.
